Professional Experience

Work Immersion

Fortuny Advertising is a creative advertising and signage company that works with well-known brands such as Angel’s Pizza, Figaro Coffee, Samsung, and DataBlitz. The company manages the entire process, from 2D and 3D layouts to printing, laser cutting, and assembly, ensuring high-quality outputs with strong attention to detail.

It fosters a professional yet collaborative environment where creativity, resourcefulness, and teamwork are highly valued, making it a place that supports both technical growth and professional development.

During my work immersion at Fortuny Advertising, I worked on a variety of tasks such as recreating 2D layouts using Photoshop and Illustrator, creating 3D models in SketchUp, and assisting in the printing and assembly of signages for clients like Angel’s Pizza, Figaro Coffee, and Samsung.

I learned how to be resourceful when given limited materials, such as searching for missing assets, identifying fonts with AI tools, and recreating graphics from scratch.

I also gained a deeper understanding of the manufacturing process of signages, the importance of precision, quality, and professionalism, as well as valuable workplace skills like teamwork, time management, and adaptability.
